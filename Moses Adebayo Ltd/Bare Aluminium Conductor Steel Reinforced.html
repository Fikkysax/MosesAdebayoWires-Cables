Bare Aluminium Conductor Steel Reinforced

Aluminum Conductor, Steel Reinforced (ACSR)

ACSR conductors are widely used in overhead power lines due to their high tensile strength and conductivity. They consist of a central steel core surrounded by layers of aluminum strands, providing both strength and electrical efficiency.
